Delete Your Chatty Account

Users can delete their account and associated data directly from the Chatty mobile application.

Steps to Delete Account

  1. Open the Chatty app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Data Deletion

Once the account is deleted, all associated user data including messages and account details will be permanently removed from our servers.
